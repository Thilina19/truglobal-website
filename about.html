Our Story

Born in New Zealand,
Built for the World

TRU Global Recruitment & Consultants was founded with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between extraordinary talent and the organizations that need them most. Based in Albany, Auckland, we have grown from a local boutique firm into a respected international recruitment partner.

Our founder, Mr. Upul Chandima — a seasoned Management Coach, Career Coach, and Marketing Coach — built TRU Global on the principles of transparency, respect, and unwavering commitment to results. Today, our network spans 7+ countries including New Zealand, Australia, Sri Lanka, UAE, Qatar, Oman, and Canada.

We don't just fill roles — we build careers and transform organizations. Every placement we make reflects our deep understanding of both employer needs and candidate aspirations.
